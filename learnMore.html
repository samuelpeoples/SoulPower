What We Do

We at Soul Power believe in a clean, renewable and efficient source of energy, the voluntary giving of souls for the betterment of all of our worlds kinds.

This energy fits perfectly inside the worlds natural life cycle, allowing all beings to give back to their communites as their final gift to the world. What could be more noble?

With an understandable stigma around such a practice we work at great lengths to remain humane, non-discriminatory and accepting of all kinds, working with all forms of guild and company, including working alongside butcher trades, allowing animals to be put down humanely while serving two vital services, and the kingdoms prison systems giving all cell members the option to provide a final act of service to their people.

As a company we want to push what we as a people can do for eachother, and work together to create a better tomorrow for one another. Will you join us?

We may be a young company, but we’re run by some of the oldest and greatest minds of our time, in only a few years overtaking all competeters in terms of efficiency and enviromental friendliness.

Did you know that the average human soul can support an entire town for three whole days?

That’s only the start, we’re constantly upping ourselves on our efficieny and reach, bringing new energy to even the most remote of villages.
